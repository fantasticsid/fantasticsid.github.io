IELTS QuizMe

Use IELTS QuizMe to enhance your vocabulary power and share with your friends the words you have difficulties understanding or memorizing. Included are 7000 words (English and its Chinese explanation) in standard IELTS test. Mark words as important or even take pictures and make flashcards to help memorize difficult words/phrases.

Features:

  • Random vocabulary test mode
  • Bookmark words/phrases
  • Associate words/phrases with pictures as you like
  • Fuzzy search words/phrases
  • Share with your friends those words you have difficulties understanding or memorizing via SMS or Email
